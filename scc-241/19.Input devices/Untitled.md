|**Symbol**|**Full Name**|**Meaning**|**Device Example**|
|---|---|---|---|
|**P**|**Position**|Measures the **absolute** location in space.|**Touchscreen:** It knows exactly which coordinate $(x, y)$ you touched.|
|**dP**|**Delta Position**|Measures a **relative** change in movement (displacement).|**Computer Mouse:** It only knows you moved 2 inches left, not where you are on the desk.|
|**F**|**Force**|Measures the **absolute** amount of pressure/force being applied.|**Digital Scale:** It measures the total weight/pressure sitting on it.|
|**dF**|**Delta Force**|Measures the **change** in the amount of force over time.|**Pressure-sensitive Pen:** It detects the increase or decrease in pressure as you draw.|
