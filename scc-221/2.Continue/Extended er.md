![[Pasted image 20251016204621.png]]“ISA” stands for **“is a” relationship** — it shows that one entity type is a **special case (subclass)** of another **general entity (superclass)**.

### Example:

- `Hourly_Emps` **is a** type of `Employee`
    
- `Contract_Emps` **is a** type of `Employee`
    

So, both subclasses **inherit** the attributes of the superclass `Employees`.
